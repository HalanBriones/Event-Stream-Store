This system consider that the courses it should be done in one day, and there are 4 different behaviors consider
1. Rushing:
  Time to finish the course: < 1 hour
2. Possibly fast/light engagement:
  Time to finish the course: >= 1 hour and <=2 hour
2. Normal(Healthy):
  Time to finish the course: >= 2 hour and <=3 hour
3. Slow(Acceptable)
  Time to finish the course:>= 3 hour and <=4 hour
4. Struggle/Distraction:
  Time to finish the course: >4
